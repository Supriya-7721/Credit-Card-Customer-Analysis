## Project Overview
This project analyzes a Credit Card Customer dataset using **Python, SQL,and Power BI** to understand customer behavior, card distribution, credit limit trends, and business performance.
The project follows a complete end-to-end data analyst workflow, including data exploration, SQL analysis, KPI creation, visualization, and business recommendations.

## Key Insights
- Mastercards customers have the highest average credit limit.
- The dataset contains **2,000 customers** with **6,146 credit cards**.
- The average credit limit is approximately **$14.35K**.
- The maximum credit limit reaches **$151K**.
- Around **89%** of the cards are chip-enabled.
- Most customers fall into the low to medium credit limit range.
- A small number of customers hold expectationally high credit limits.
- Customer account openings vary across different months.

# Business Recommendations
- Promote premium credit cards to high-value customers.
- Increase marketing campaigns for medium credit limit customers.
- Continue encouraging chip-enabled card adoption.
- Focus marketing efforts during months with higher account openings.
- Monitor customers with very high credit limits for effective risk management.
- Develop personalized offers based on customer segments.
